About the Department

The Department of Computer Science was established in 1992 under the Faculty of Science at Egerton University. Since its inception, the department has grown into a hub of innovation, research, and training in information and communication technology (ICT).

History

The department was created to respond to the growing demand for computer science professionals in Kenya and beyond. Over the years, it has expanded its academic programs, facilities, and staff to keep up with global trends in computing.

Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in computer science and ICT education in Kenya and beyond.

Mission

To provide quality education in computing and information technology, conduct innovative research, and produce graduates who can solve real-world problems.

Core Values

  • Excellence
  • Innovation
  • Integrity
  • Teamwork
  • Professionalism
