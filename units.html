Units Offered

The Bachelor of Science in Applied Computer Science program is structured into four academic years. Below is a summary of the major units taught in each year. (Note: The exact course structure may vary; confirm with the department for details.)

Year 1

  • Introduction to Programming (C)
  • Computer Organization
  • Logic and Set Theory
  • Fundamentals of ICT
  • Communication Skills
  • Probability and Statistics

Year 2

  • Object-Oriented Programming (C++)
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Database Management Systems
  • Operating Systems
  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Systems Analysis and Design

Year 3

  • Software Engineering
  • Computer Networks
  • Web Development (Frontend and Backend)
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Research Methods
  • Artificial Intelligence (Introduction)

Year 4

  • Mobile Application Development
  • Cyber Security
  • Advanced Database Systems
  • Project Management in IT
  • Final Year Project (Research and System Development)
