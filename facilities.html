Facilities

The Department of Computer Science provides modern learning and research facilities to ensure students gain both theoretical knowledge and practical computing skills.

Computer Laboratories

  • Programming Lab: Equipped with PCs for C, C++, Java, and Python programming.
  • Networking Lab: Used for networking simulations, router configuration, and cybersecurity training.
  • Multimedia Lab: For graphic design, web development, and multimedia projects.

Software Resources

  • Database software (MySQL, Oracle, MS Access)
  • Programming IDEs (Code::Blocks, Eclipse, Visual Studio, NetBeans)
  • Web Development Tools (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP environments)
  • Networking tools (Cisco Packet Tracer, Wireshark)

Library and E-Resources

Students have access to Egerton University Library, which provides textbooks, research journals, and online databases relevant to computer science and ICT.

Other Facilities

  • Lecture halls with multimedia projectors
  • Wi-Fi access within the department
  • Department office and student helpdesk
