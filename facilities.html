Our Facilities

Programming Lab

Equipped with modern PCs for C, C++, Java, and Python programming exercises. Features high-speed internet and specialized software for software development.

Networking Lab

Dedicated for networking simulations, router configuration, and cybersecurity training. Includes Cisco equipment and specialized network analysis tools.

Multimedia Lab

Supports graphic design, web development, and multimedia projects. Equipped with high-end computers and creative software suites.

Department of Computer Science

Egerton University is committed to excellence in computer science education, research, and innovation. Our department continues to be a leader in technological education in Kenya and beyond.
