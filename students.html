Students

The Department of Computer Science is home to vibrant and innovative students who participate in academic, co-curricular, and community activities. Our students are passionate about technology, problem-solving, and using ICT to improve lives.

Clubs and Associations

  • Computer Science Students Association (CSSA): A student-led group that organizes coding bootcamps, tech talks, and mentorship programs.
  • Innovation and Robotics Club: A space for experimenting with AI, IoT, and robotics projects.
  • Hackathon Teams: Students regularly form teams to compete in local and international hackathons.

Student Projects and Innovations

Each year, students work on creative projects that demonstrate their computing skills. Some notable areas include:

  • Mobile applications for health and education.
  • Web-based business management systems.
  • Artificial intelligence tools for local solutions.
  • Community outreach through digital literacy programs.

Achievements

Students from the department have won recognition in programming contests, presented research at conferences, and successfully launched startups. Their contributions continue to shape both local and international ICT spaces.
