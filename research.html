Research

Research is a core pillar of the Department of Computer Science. Faculty and students work together on projects that solve real-world challenges through technology and innovation.

Research Areas

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity and Data Privacy
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Mobile and Web Application Development
  • Computer Networks and Cloud Computing
  • ICT for Agriculture, Health, and Education

Ongoing Projects

  • Development of e-Learning platforms for remote education.
  • Smart farming applications using IoT sensors and data analytics.
  • Cybersecurity awareness and training systems for schools and SMEs.
  • AI-powered chatbots for student support and university services.

Collaborations

The department actively collaborates with industry partners, research institutions, and government agencies. Partnerships provide students with opportunities for internships, mentorship, and applied research.

  • Local tech startups and software companies
  • Kenya Education Network (KENET)
  • International research organizations and universities
