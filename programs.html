Academic Programs

The Department of Applied Computer Science offers programs designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical ICT skills, preparing them for careers in software development, networking, databases, cybersecurity, and research.

Undergraduate Programs

  • Bachelor of Science in Applied Computer Science

    A four-year program that covers programming, algorithms, software engineering, networking, databases, artificial intelligence, and emerging technologies. Students undertake practical projects and a final year project to apply their skills.

Postgraduate Programs

Postgraduate programs are being developed to offer advanced training in computer science and related areas. Check the department office for updates on available MSc programs.

Service Courses

The department also offers computing courses to other faculties at Egerton University. These include:

  • Introduction to Programming
  • Computer Applications
  • Database Fundamentals
