Welcome to Our Department

Our Mission

The Department of Computer and Applied Computer Science at Egerton University is a center of excellence in computing education, research, and innovation. Our programs are designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the rapidly evolving field of computer science.

Quality Education
Research Excellence

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to providing quality education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing students for successful careers in technology. With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and strong industry partnerships, we provide an environment that fosters academic excellence and professional growth.

Our students work on real-world projects that prepare them for the tech industry.

About Our Department

History

Established in 1992, the Department of Computer and Applied Computer Science at Egerton University has been at the forefront of computing education and research in Kenya. Our department was created to respond to the growing demand for computer science professionals in Kenya and beyond.

Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in computer science and ICT education, research, and innovation in Kenya and beyond, producing graduates who are globally competitive and capable of driving technological advancement.

Mission

To provide quality education in computing and information technology, conduct innovative research that addresses societal challenges, and produce graduates who can solve real-world problems using technology.

Academic Programs

BSc Computer Science

A comprehensive four-year program covering programming, algorithms, software engineering, networking, databases, and artificial intelligence. Students engage in hands-on labs, industry attachments, and a final year project to apply their knowledge.

Career Opportunities

  • Software Developer
  • Systems Analyst
  • Database Administrator
  • Network Engineer
Program Details

BSc Applied Computer Science

Focuses on the application of computing in real-world settings, integrating programming, database systems, information management, and applied technologies to solve societal and industrial challenges.

Career Opportunities

  • IT Consultant
  • Applications Developer
  • Business Analyst
  • IT Project Manager

Research & Innovation

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Applications in health, agriculture, and business analytics. Our researchers are developing AI solutions for crop disease detection and predictive healthcare models.

Key Projects: AI-powered crop disease detection system for smallholder farmers; Machine learning models for predicting student academic performance.

Data Science & Big Data Analytics

Leveraging large datasets for decision-making in various sectors including finance, public health, and environmental monitoring.

Key Projects: Predictive analytics for financial markets; Big data solutions for healthcare management.

Cybersecurity & Networks

Ensuring secure digital environments and resilient infrastructures through advanced cryptographic techniques and network protocols.

Key Projects: Development of a secure e-voting system using blockchain technology; Network intrusion detection systems.
